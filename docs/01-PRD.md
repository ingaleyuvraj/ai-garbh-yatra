## A. Product vision

> "प्रत्येक मराठी आईला आणि तिच्या जोडीदाराला, गर्भधारणेच्या प्रवासात शांत, सकारात्मक आणि विश्वासार्ह दैनंदिन सोबत देणे — संस्कृतीशी जोडलेली पण आधुनिक, आणि नेहमी डॉक्टरांच्या सल्ल्याचा आदर करणारी."

**Vision statement (EN):** To be the most trusted, calming, Marathi-first daily companion for pregnancy wellbeing — blending positive psychology, gentle Indian cultural rituals (garbh samvad, mantra, music), structured daily routine, and partner participation — strictly as a wellness and educational support tool, never as a medical or diagnostic product.

**What we are:** Wellness · educational · spiritual support · daily routine · prenatal engagement.
**What we are NOT:** Medical diagnosis · symptom checker · gender prediction · superstition · medical advice replacement.

### Product pillars
1. **शांतता (Calm):** Reduce anxiety through music, breathing, meditation, affirmations.
2. **जोड (Bonding):** Garbh samvad and partner participation to deepen connection.
3. **दिनचर्या (Routine):** A gentle, achievable "आजचा दिवस" plan every day.
4. **ज्ञान (Knowledge):** Trimester-wise, non-diagnostic educational content.
5. **विश्वास (Trust):** Safety messaging, privacy, doctor-respect throughout.

## C. User personas

### Persona 1 — स्वप्नाली (Planning couple, 28)
- **Stage:** Planning conception with husband.
- **Tech:** Comfortable smartphone user, uses WhatsApp, YouTube, Instagram.
- **Goals:** Prepare mind & body, build healthy routine, reduce anxiety.
- **Needs:** Pre-conception wellness, partner involvement, light guidance.
- **Pain:** Most apps are English-only and medical/clinical in tone.

### Persona 2 — प्राजक्ता (1st trimester, 31)
- **Stage:** 9 weeks pregnant, first child.
- **Tech:** Moderate; relies on family for some decisions.
- **Goals:** Manage nausea-anxiety emotionally, daily reassurance, what's safe.
- **Needs:** Calm content, mood support, "is this normal?" reassurance with doctor nudges.
- **Pain:** Information overload, fear, conflicting advice.

### Persona 3 — मनीषा (3rd trimester, 34)
- **Stage:** 33 weeks, second child, working.
- **Goals:** Garbh samvad, sleep, prepare for delivery emotionally, bonding.
- **Needs:** Short daily rituals, audio she can play hands-free, reminders.
- **Pain:** Limited time, wants offline audio during commute.

### Persona 4 — सुनीता (First-time smartphone user, 26, semi-urban)
- **Tech:** Low-tech, large fonts needed, prefers audio over reading.
- **Goals:** Simple daily activity, devotional listening, feel supported.
- **Needs:** Big tap targets, voice/audio, minimal text, very simple navigation.
- **Pain:** Complex UIs, English text, small fonts.

### Persona 5 — विकास (Participating partner, 33)
- **Goals:** Be involved, talk to baby, support wife.
- **Needs:** Partner tasks, garbh samvad scripts, gentle nudges.
- **Pain:** Doesn't know "what to do" during pregnancy.

## F. Success metrics (KPIs)

- **Activation:** % completing onboarding + first "आजचा दिवस" (target > 70%).
- **D1 / D7 / D30 retention** (target D7 > 35%, D30 > 20%).
- **Daily active engagement:** avg. routine cards completed/day.
- **Audio plays/user/week** (garbh samvad + mantra + music).
- **Reminder opt-in rate** and reminder-driven opens.
- **Partner linkage rate.**
- **Offline downloads/user.**
- **Subscription conversion** (freemium → premium).
- **NPS / "felt calmer" qualitative survey.**

## G. Monetization (freemium model)

**Free tier (always):**
- आजचा दिवस daily card, 1 affirmation/day, basic garbh samvad, limited mantra/music, water/mood/journal, reminders, weekly summary, disclaimer.

**Premium (गर्भयात्रा प्लस — subscription):**
- Full mantra/prayer + calm music + meditation libraries.
- Full offline download packs (per trimester).
- Personalized daily schedule + smart reminders (Phase 2).
- Partner premium features + shared journal.
- Ad-free, plus seasonal special audio (festival mantras, lullabies).
- Expert sessions (Phase 3).

**Pricing (India-first, indicative):**
- Monthly: ₹99 · Quarterly: ₹249 · Full-pregnancy pass (9 months): ₹599 · Lifetime: ₹999.
- Free trial: 7 days. Family/partner add-on bundled free.

**Other (ethical only):** Optional non-intrusive partnerships (verified prenatal product/clinic listings) — clearly labeled, never disguised as medical advice. No targeted ads on sensitive health data.

## H. Risk & compliance notes

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Perceived as medical advice | Persistent disclaimer; non-diagnostic language; "डॉक्टरांचा सल्ला घ्या" on every health section; content review by a medical advisor |
| Gender selection / superstition pressure | Hard policy ban; content guidelines; reject any such submissions; in-app reporting |
| Sensitive health data privacy | Minimize data; encrypt at rest/in transit; explicit consent; data export/delete; comply with India DPDP Act 2023 |
| Harmful/unverified claims | Editorial review workflow + medical advisor sign-off before publish |
| Emotional distress / mental health | Provide helpline info; never diagnose; gentle escalation copy to consult professionals |
| Play Store medical policy | Position as "wellness/education", avoid medical claims in listing, include disclaimer in description |
| Accessibility exclusion | Large fonts, high contrast, audio-first, simple nav (see design system) |
| Content accuracy across trimesters | Versioned content with review dates; advisor approval; clear "general information" labeling |
| Children's data (N/A) | App is for adults; age-gate at onboarding |

**Legal & policy alignment:** India DPDP Act 2023, Google Play Health & Medical + Subscriptions policies, PCPNDT Act awareness (strict no sex-determination/selection content). Add Privacy Policy + Terms (see [docs/08-play-store-and-legal.md](08-play-store-and-legal.md)).

## I. Out of scope (explicitly)

- Diagnosis, symptom checking, medication advice, dosage.
- Gender prediction/selection, sex-determination.
- Astrology-based health claims, superstition-based "remedies".
- Replacing doctor visits, lab interpretation.
- Telemedicine/prescriptions (may be revisited as vetted partner integration only).
